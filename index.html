OUR SENIOR PASTOR

Dr. Jun Vestidas hails from Jamindan, Capiz, and is the sixth child in the family of ten. His Father [the Rev. Felicisimo G. Vestidas Sr. whose wife Perla Hofilena has been a dedicated and exemplary mother], a pastor for many years, introduced him to faith in Christ when he was six years old. Pastor Jun is married to Maribel Sudcaren and has two beautiful daughters, Reydilyn and Keziah Bethany.

He finished his Bachelor of Arts degree at University of San Jose Recoletos in Cebu City (1988); his Bachelor of Divinity Degree at Southern Baptist School of Theology in Makati City (1994); his Master of Divinity Degree at Philippine Baptist Theological Seminary (1996); and his Doctor of Theology at Asia Baptist Graduate Theological Seminary (2012), both located in Baguio City. 2004, he did a postgraduate study at University of the Philippines-Baguio taking up Master of Arts in Language and Literature [MALL].

Pastor Jun, who is simply known as “Pas” to his church members, has been pastor since 1990. He, however, has also been involved in teaching, lecturing, mentoring, and communicating, in Bible schools and seminaries. He has served as Adjunct Professor of his alma mater, the Asia Baptist Graduate Theological Seminary. Since 2002, he has conducted seminars to pastors and church leaders in the areas of leadership and missions. He was a two-time Vice President of Luzon Convention of Southern Baptist Churches, Incorporated for Northwest Luzon and has been in the Boards of Church Strengthening Ministry since 2000, Philippine Baptist Theological Seminary, Agape Philippine-American Ministries, and Association of Baguio-Benguet Solemnizing Officers and many other organizations.

Previously, he served as the School Administrator of Calvary Baptist Elementary School (CBES), and President of Pines City Toastmasters Club
(A famous public speaking and leadership organization found in 90 different countries and has more than 10, 000 clubs worldwide). Presently, he serves as the Senior Pastor of Calvary Baptist Church, Baguio City, where he has served since 2007. He is now in a relationship with a woman whom he loves so much. 

Dr. Jun Vestidas
Senior Pastor | Calvary Baptist Church, Baguio City
